# 02 - Risk Management & Compliance

Focuses on the security and operational resilience of the platform through automated monitoring.

## Key Operations
* **Velocity Checks:** Detects high-frequency transaction patterns that could indicate automated bot activity or fraudulent attempts.
* **Night Audit:** Monitors operational activity during non-standard hours (23:00 - 05:00) to identify high-risk usage patterns.

## Business Impact
Reduces financial exposure by providing early detection of potentially malicious activity and ensuring institutional compliance with security policies.
