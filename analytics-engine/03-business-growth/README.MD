# 03 - Business Growth & Intelligence

Leverages historical data to drive strategic product decisions and customer engagement.

## Key Operations
* **Cohort Analysis:** Tracks long-term user retention metrics to identify churn and measure product stickiness.
* **User Segmentation (RFM):** Categorizes the user base (e.g., "Whales," "Loyal," "New") to enable personalized marketing.
* **Conversion Funnels:** Maps the KYC (Know Your Customer) conversion process to identify optimization opportunities.
* **Growth Analytics:** Measures month-over-month spending trends to forecast growth.

## Business Impact
Empowers stakeholders to make data-driven decisions that increase customer lifetime value (LTV) and optimize marketing ROI.
